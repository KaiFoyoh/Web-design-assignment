Here's Everything you need to know about us

The S.L.F.A is the governing body of Football in Sierra Leone. Founded in 1960, and afflitated to the FIFA the same year. It organizes and runs the national leagues,and the national Football teams, including the under-17, under-20, under-23, and the senior team.
